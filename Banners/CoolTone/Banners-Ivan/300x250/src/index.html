Uses and Important Safety Information CoolTone® is FDA-cleared to strengthen, tone, and firm muscles of the abdomen, buttocks, and thighs. The CoolTone® procedure is not for everyone. Tell your doctor about all your medical conditions. Some common side effects include muscle, joint or tendon pain, muscle spasm, and skin redness. Ask your doctor if CoolTone® is right for you.
